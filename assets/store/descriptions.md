## Long Description (Steam BBCode)

[h1]ArcaneGaunt[/h1]

[b]A first-person wizard roguelike.[/b] Pick your spell, enter the arena, and fight through escalating waves of monsters. Every run is a fresh challenge: procedurally generated arena layouts, randomised wave modifiers, and a reward system that pushes you to adapt your build on the fly.

[list]
[*] [b]Six spell archetypes[/b] — Arcane Bolt, Fireball, Frost Bolt, Poison Bolt, Chain Lightning, and Meteor. Each plays differently and has its own branching upgrade tree.
[*] [b]Deep per-run progression[/b] — Clear waves for gold, spend it on spell upgrades and between-wave services. Unlock auto-cast to add a second spell to your loadout.
[*] [b]Build-defining relics[/b] — Rare passive rewards that change how you play: empower spells after a perfect block, reward close-range casting, or trade max health for raw power.
[*] [b]Block & parry[/b] — Hold to block incoming damage. Time it perfectly to reflect projectiles back at your enemies.
[*] [b]Dynamic encounters[/b] — Six enemy types, three boss variants, and wave modifiers (Swift Horde, Armored, Volatile, Regenerating, Elite Vanguard) keep every wave unpredictable.
[*] [b]Arena variety[/b] — Each run rebuilds the arena with lane blockers, cover clusters, gate walls, and phase-rift hazard strips. No two runs play the same arena layout.
[/list]

[h2]Controls[/h2]
[list]
[*] WASD — Move
[*] Mouse — Look
[*] Left Click — Cast spell
[*] Right Click (hold) — Block / parry
[*] Space — Jump
[*] Shift / Q — Blink (short dash)
[*] 1–6 / Mouse Wheel — Select spell
[*] Esc — Pause / release mouse
[/list]

Full gamepad support included.

[h2]Platform[/h2]
Windows only. Requires a 64-bit processor and operating system.
